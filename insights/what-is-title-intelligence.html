What Is Title Intelligence in Real Estate?

Title Intelligence is risk visibility and decision posture for title and settlement workflows—earlier signals, clearer outcomes.

Outcomes-only: Decision framing, not implementation details.

Key ideas

  • Earlier risk visibility reduces surprise delays.
  • Clear-to-close pacing aligns teams on next actions.
  • Audit-ready rationale documents decisions.
  • Partner coordination across stakeholders.
  • Enterprise delivery: scoped and governed.

Delivery boundary: Detailed architectures, templates, simulations, dashboards, and runbooks are delivered via engagement through the MBCC Delivery Vault.
