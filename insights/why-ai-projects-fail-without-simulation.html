Why AI Projects Fail Without Simulation

Simulation validates ROI and risk before production commitments—without exposing implementation assets.

Outcomes-only: Decision framing, not implementation details.

Key ideas

  • Simulation prevents confusing demos with readiness.
  • Clarifies constraints and success criteria early.
  • Reveals edge cases: latency, policy failures, handoffs, cost spikes.
  • Supports governance decisions before deployment.
  • Creates stakeholder alignment around outcomes.
