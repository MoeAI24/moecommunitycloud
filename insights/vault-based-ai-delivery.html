Why Enterprises Need Vault-Based AI Delivery

Vault-based delivery protects client data and provider IP while enabling measurable outcomes.

Outcomes-only: Decision framing, not implementation details.

Key ideas

  • Reduce IP leakage by keeping detailed assets private.
  • Provision access per engagement scope and team.
  • Ship assets with controls and runbooks, not fragments.
  • Speed execution once decisions are made.
  • Professionalize delivery with clear boundaries.

Delivery boundary: Detailed architectures, templates, simulations, dashboards, and runbooks are delivered via engagement through the MBCC Delivery Vault.
