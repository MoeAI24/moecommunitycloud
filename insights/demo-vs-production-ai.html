Demos vs Production AI

A demo sells the idea. Production delivers value—reliably, securely, and repeatedly.

Outcomes-only: Decision framing, not implementation details.

Key ideas

  • Production needs guardrails: access control, logging, fail-safes.
  • Operational posture: monitoring, incident response, cost controls.
  • Separate outcomes from build details to protect IP.
  • Use a paid review → sprint → platform build progression.
  • Production wins markets; demos win meetings.
