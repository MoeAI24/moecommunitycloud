What is Vapor Cloud?

Vapor Cloud is the operating mindset behind MBCC: design systems that work locally, in hybrid mode, and on any major cloud. Instead of getting locked into one provider, we build for portability and resilience.

That shows up in the playbooks as simulations, architectures, and blueprints that can run in Google Cloud first – then extend to other environments when needed.

What is Agent-Ops™?

Agent-Ops is the DevOps of agentic AI: a framework for designing, observing, and controlling agents like real software – with blueprints, chains, risk models, and replay logs.

The Agent-Ops Playbook gives you a way to explain agents to executives, design them responsibly, and sell them as a real offer – with dashboards and guardrails instead of chaos.

Why Playbooks?

A playbook is more than a deck. It’s a reusable system: story, architecture diagrams, simulations, scoping tools, and a proposal generator tied together.

That means you can walk into a room with confidence, show “what good looks like” on screen, then turn the conversation into a real scoped engagement before you leave.
