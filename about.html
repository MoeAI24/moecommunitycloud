What is Vapor Cloud?

Vapor Cloud is an operating mindset: design systems that can run locally, in hybrid mode, and on any major cloud. No lock-in, no brittle architectures – just resilient patterns that travel.

For enterprises, that means production architectures that fit your governance and security model. For VCDLs, it means you can walk into any account with a portable story that works beyond one vendor logo.

What is Agent-Ops™?

Agent-Ops is the DevOps of agentic AI: a way to design, observe, and control agents like real software – with blueprints, chains, risk models, and replay logs instead of “magic.”

For enterprises, it turns agents into governed systems that security and ops can trust. For VCDLs, it turns agent projects into a repeatable offer backed by simulations and runbooks.

Why Playbooks?

A playbook is more than a deck. It’s a reusable system: narrative, architecture diagrams, simulations, scoping tools, and a proposal generator that all line up.

For enterprises, that means you see the architecture, the risk posture, and the path to production before you sign. For VCDLs, it means you show up with a complete offer, not just slides and hope.
