Partner Application

Apply to Become an Authorized MBCC Delivery Partner

We onboard a limited number of partners each quarter. This application determines eligibility for certification, licensing, and enterprise delivery under Moe Community Cloud standards.

Who should apply

  • Consulting firms expanding into AI or finance ops
  • Operators seeking a repeatable delivery system
  • Partners who want licensed frameworks—not raw IP

Who should not apply

  • Resellers looking for source code
  • Individuals seeking certification only
  • Teams unwilling to follow delivery standards

What acceptance includes

  • Structured onboarding & training
  • Certification & delivery entitlement
  • Enterprise proof artifacts
  • Clear licensing boundaries

What acceptance does not include

  • Ownership of MBCC IP
  • Unrestricted resale rights
  • Custom exceptions to governance
