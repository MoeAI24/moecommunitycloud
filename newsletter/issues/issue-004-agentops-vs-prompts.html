ISSUE 004 AGENTOPS VS PROMPTS

Agent Ops Beats Prompt Engineering Every Time

2025-12-19
Join → Back

Great prompts don’t save broken systems. Prompting is useful — but operations is what scales.

What breaks in real life

  • Inputs shift, behavior drifts
  • Tool permissions get messy
  • Auditors ask “who approved that?”
  • Incidents need traceability

What AgentOps adds

  • Logs and traceability
  • RBAC and permission boundaries
  • Change control and versioning
  • Evidence generation for review
  • Monitoring and escalation paths
