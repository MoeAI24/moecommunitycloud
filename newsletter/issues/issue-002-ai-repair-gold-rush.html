AI Repair Is the Next Gold Rush

2025-12-17
Join → Back

The second wave of AI money isn’t adoption. It’s **repair**.

The market is filling with systems that work in a demo but degrade under real usage. The winners will be operators who can diagnose, stabilize, and harden AI into something enterprises can trust.

What “repair” includes

  • Evaluation harnesses and regression tests
  • Logging and traceability across tool calls
  • Guardrails and permissioned actions
  • Cost controls and performance baselines
  • Evidence packages for security and compliance reviews

Why it pays

Repair converts prototypes into controlled production systems — the exact thing enterprises will pay to approve and scale.
