The 30-Minute AgentOps Audit

2026-01-24 • Agent Ops • MBCC

If you only have 30 minutes to assess an AI system, you can still find the truth: who owns it, what it does, and where it breaks.

Partner Path → Use Free Tools → Done-for-you →

What this issue covers

  • The 10 questions that reveal whether the system is operable
  • A quick way to detect hidden risk (drift, cost spikes, unsafe actions)
  • How to turn your findings into an executive-forwardable summary
Takeaway: A 30-minute audit beats a 30-day argument. Clarity creates speed.

The 10-question audit

Ask: Who owns it? What does it touch? What actions can it take? Where are logs? Who can change it? What’s the rollback?

If you can’t answer those, you don’t have a system — you have a demo.

The fast risk scan

Check three signals: latency, error rate, and cost per request.

Then check drift: are outputs changing under the same inputs?

Turn it into proof

Write one page: what it does, top risks, controls, and next actions.

Executives forward one page. Auditors verify details later.

Quick action plan

  1. Run: Cost Reality Checker + Uptime Risk Score.
  2. Create a one-page proof pack: what changed, who approved it, what guardrails exist.
  3. Repeat weekly: measure → fix top bottleneck → document → repeat.
