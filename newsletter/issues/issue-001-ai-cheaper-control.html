ISSUE 001 AI CHEAPER CONTROL

AI Is Getting Cheaper — Control Is Getting More Expensive

2025-12-16
Join → Back

Most people are watching the price of intelligence drop. Enterprises are watching something else: the price of **control**.

When generation becomes cheap, the premium shifts to the operating layer that keeps systems safe, explainable, and operable in production.

The new premium

  • Governance: who can do what, when, and why
  • Evidence: logs, evaluations, approvals, and change history
  • Operations: monitoring, escalation, and recovery paths
  • Boundaries: kill switches, human review gates, permissioned tool access

What to do now

Treat control as a deliverable. If you can’t explain an agent’s behavior, you can’t scale it. If you can’t prove it, you can’t sell it.
