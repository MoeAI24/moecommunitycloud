What it is

Govern how AI acts — before it acts

The Control Plane is an operating layer that supervises multi-agent systems in-flight: authority, boundaries, observability, and intervention. It sits above tools and models so your outcomes stay durable through vendor churn.

Not a phase — an operating condition

Once agents touch production workflows, governance must be enforceable while the system runs. If shutdown authority and boundaries cannot be applied in real time, innovation erodes quietly.

Outcome pillars

Coordinated execution

Multiple agents work together under enforced order, escalation paths, and quality gates.

Human authority

AI proposes; humans retain control. Decisions can be paused, rerouted, or stopped.

Observability

Actions are traceable and reviewable so leaders can intervene with confidence.

Where it applies

Executive decision intelligence

Briefings that surface risks, tradeoffs, and “what-if” scenarios before decisions land.

Governance & compliance

Operational enforcement of boundaries instead of policy documents that sit on a shelf.

Incident response

Detection → containment → executive comms — under defined authority and audit trails.
