How it works (conceptual)

Above tools — not replacing them

Tools and models execute. The Control Plane governs. It defines allowed behaviors, escalation paths, and intervention authority so multi-agent systems stay safe and durable.

Lifecycle

1) Design

Define objectives, authority boundaries, and success criteria.

2) Simulate

Stress-test workflows and failure modes before production exposure.

3) Deploy

Roll out with clear ownership, auditability, and escalation paths.

4) Observe

Track actions, exceptions, and drift — continuously.

5) Intervene

Pause, reroute, or stop workflows when risk rises.

6) Improve

Turn incidents and feedback into stronger guardrails and runbooks.
