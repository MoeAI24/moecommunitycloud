What this playbook does

Turn store data into a simple money story

The Retail AI Revenue Playbook helps you walk executives from “we have POS and cameras” to “here is the projected revenue lift, payback period, and rollout plan.”

  • Shows revenue impact from AI on traffic, conversion, and average basket.
  • Gives you a live simulation you can run in front of the client.
  • Outputs a proposal summary you can paste into PowerPoint, Docs, or email.

How to use this in consulting

In-room workshop flow

  1. 01 · Frame the problem – shrink margin, rising costs, fragmented data.
  2. 02 · Input their numbers into the simulation (traffic, conversion, basket, stores).
  3. 03 · Tune AI uplift assumptions until the sponsor says “that looks realistic.”
  4. 04 · Show payback period and “what if we scale from 10 → 100 stores.”
  5. 05 · Click into Proposal Generator and build a one-page summary together.

The goal: leave the room with a clear money narrative and a mutually agreed pilot scope that can move into procurement.
